HIRDLS_L2

Variables

The table below lists the variables that are present in the HARP product that results from an ingestion of HIRDLS_L2 data.

field name type dimensions unit description
datetime double {time} seconds since 2000-01-01 time of the measurement (in seconds since 2000-01-01 00:00:00)
longitude double {time} degree_east tangent longitude
latitude double {time} degree_north tangent latitude
altitude double {time, vertical} m altitude per profile level
pressure double {vertical} hPa pressure per profile level
CCl3F_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv CCl3F (CFC-11) volume mixing ratio
CCl3F_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the CCl3F (CFC-11) volume mixing ratio
CCl2F2_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv CCl2F2 (CFC-12) volume mixing ratio
CCl2F2_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the CCl2F2 (CFC-12) volume mixing ratio
CH4_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv CH4 volume mixing ratio
CH4_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the CH4 volume mixing ratio
ClONO2_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv ClONO2 volume mixing ratio
ClONO2_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the ClONO2 volume mixing ratio
H2O_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv H2O volume mixing ratio
H2O_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the H2O volume mixing ratio
HNO3_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv HNO3 volume mixing ratio
HNO3_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the HNO3 volume mixing ratio
N2O_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv N2O volume mixing ratio
N2O_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the N2O volume mixing ratio
N2O5_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv N2O5 volume mixing ratio
N2O5_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the N2O5 volume mixing ratio
NO2_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv NO2 volume mixing ratio
NO2_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the NO2 volume mixing ratio
O3_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv O3 volume mixing ratio
O3_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time, vertical} ppv uncertainty of the O3 volume mixing ratio
temperature double {time, vertical} K temperature
temperature_uncertainty double {time, vertical} K uncertainty of the temperature
index int32 {time}   zero-based index of the sample within the source product
