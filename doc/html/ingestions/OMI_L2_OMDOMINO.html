OMI_L2_OMDOMINO

Variables

The table below lists the variables that are present in the HARP product that results from an ingestion of OMI_L2_OMDOMINO data.

field name type dimensions unit description
datetime double {time} seconds since 2000-01-01 time of the measurement
longitude double {time} degree_east longitude of the ground pixel center
latitude double {time} degree_north latitude of the ground pixel center
longitude_bounds double {time, 4} degree_east longitudes of the ground pixel corners
latitude_bounds double {time, 4} degree_north latitudes of the ground pixel corners
solar_zenith_angle double {time} degree solar zenith angle at WGS84 ellipsoid for center co-ordinate of the ground pixel
solar_azimuth_angle double {time} degree solar azimuth angle at WGS84 ellipsoid for center co-ordinate of the ground pixel, defined East-of-North
viewing_zenith_angle double {time} degree viewing zenith angle at WGS84 ellipsoid for center co-ordinate of the ground pixel
viewing_azimuth_angle double {time} degree viewing azimuth angle at WGS84 ellipsoid for center co-ordinate of the ground pixel, defined East-of-North
NO2_column_number_density double {time} molec/cm^2 NO2 vertical column density
NO2_column_number_density_uncertainty double {time} molec/cm^2 uncertainty of the NO2 vertical column density
tropospheric_NO2_column_number_density double {time} molec/cm^2 NO2 tropospheric column density
tropospheric_NO2_column_number_density_uncertainty double {time} molec/cm^2 uncertainty of the NO2 tropospheric column density
tropospheric_NO2_column_number_density_validity int8 {time}   flags describing the NO2 tropospheric column processing quality
cloud_fraction double {time} 1 effective cloud fraction
cloud_fraction_uncertainty double {time} 1 uncertainty of the effective cloud fraction
cloud_pressure double {time} hPa effective cloud pressure
cloud_pressure_uncertainty double {time} hPa uncertainty of the effective cloud pressure
index int32 {time}   zero-based index of the sample within the source product
