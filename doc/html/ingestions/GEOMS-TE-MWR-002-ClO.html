GEOMS-TE-MWR-002-ClO

Variables

The table below lists the variables that are present in the HARP product that results from an ingestion of GEOMS-TE-MWR-002-ClO data.

field name type dimensions unit description
instrument_name string     name of the instrument
site_name string     name of the site at which the instrument is located
instrument_latitude double   degree_north latitude of the instrument
instrument_longitude double   degree_east longitude of the instrument
instrument_altitude double   m altitude of the instrument
datetime double {time} days since 2000-01-01 time of the measurement
viewing_azimuth_angle double {time} degree viewing azimuth angle
viewing_zenith_angle double {time} degree mean viewing zenith angle
solar_zenith_angle double {time} degree mean solar zenith angle
datetime_start double {time} days since 2000-01-01 start time of the measurement
datetime_stop double {time} days since 2000-01-01 stop time of the measurement
altitude double {vertical} m altitude
pressure double {time, vertical} hPa independent pressure profile
temperature double {time, vertical} K independent temperature profile
ClO_volume_mixing_ratio double {time, vertical} ppv ClO volume mixing ratio
ClO_volume_mixing_ratio_stdev_random double {time, vertical} ppv random standard deviation of the ClO volume mixin
ClO_volume_mixing_ratio_stdev_systematic double {time, vertical, vertical} ppv systematic standard deviation of the ClO volume m
ClO_volume_mixing_ratio_apriori double {time, vertical} ppv a priori ClO volume mixing ratio
ClO_volume_mixing_ratio_avk double {time, vertical, vertical} 1 averaging kernel for the ClO volume mixing ratio
H2O_column_number_density double {time} molec/cm2 derived integrated water vapor partial column from retrieval
index int32 {time}   zero-based index of the sample within the source product
